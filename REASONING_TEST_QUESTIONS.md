- **Low Recall Confidence**: Limited direct memory associations

- **Creative Analogies**: Multiple metaphorical connections
- **Counterfactual Reasoning**: "What if" scenarios explored

### Testing Protocol
1. **Run Question**: Process through Melvin's blended reasoning
2. **Analyze Tracks**: Check recall vs exploration weighting
3. **Evaluate Response**: Assess creativity and coherence
4. **Track Confidence**: Monitor how confidence affects weighting
5. **Context Update**: Maintain conversation flow
